# Legal completion checklist

The in-app privacy, terms, and acceptable-use pages are operational templates,
not final legal advice.

Before sale or public launch, the responsible owner must:

- replace `VITE_LEGAL_NAME`, `VITE_LEGAL_EMAIL`, `VITE_SUPPORT_EMAIL`, and the
  effective date with monitored buyer-owned details;
- identify the controller/processor roles and every infrastructure provider;
- state processing purposes, legal bases, retention periods, data regions,
  international transfers, and user rights for the target markets;
- choose and disclose a minimum age and parental-consent process where needed;
- define moderation, appeals, law-enforcement requests, and emergency handling;
- define breach notification, security reporting, and incident ownership;
- state whether existing production user data is included in the sale and
  obtain any notice or consent required for that transfer;
- ensure marketing says messages are encrypted in transit, not E2EE;
- execute a written IP assignment or source-code sale agreement;
- obtain qualified legal review in the seller's and buyer's jurisdictions.
